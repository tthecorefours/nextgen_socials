Mousse Cake Moment – Layers of Pure Chocolate Bliss

The top glossy chocolate ganache reflects the light so well and it promises a smooth velvety finish all the chocolate lovers are yearning for. Divide it in half and the strata speak volumes. Every bite is easy to melt and your palate is covered with smooth and luxurious chocolate, not too heavy.

It is the texture that is really outstanding. It is creamy, soft almost cloud-like, and at the same time, plastered to such an extent that it forms a beautiful shape. Served a little on the chilled side it is still more satisfying and each forkful is the ideal melt-in-the-mouth experience.

Final Rating: 10/10 - Rich, silky, and absolutely unforgettable.

Homemade Healthy Chicken Pizza - Clean, Comforting & Satisfying

This homemade chicken pizza is an indication that you can still have the best meal, comfort food, and still not sacrifice its goodness. Handcrafted and full of nutritious food, it is the type of food that makes one feel luxurious and at the same time, guilt free.

The base seems to be baked to perfection, it is firm enough to support all the toppings but soft enough to chew without crushing. It is really thickly covered with chicken topping (which is well seasoned and cooked just right tender, juicy and flavored). The melted cheese provides that homely taste of pizza, a stretch with each bite and a perfect complement to the smoky, gently spicy chicken.

Final Rating: 9.5/10 - Proof that homemade and healthy can still taste amazing.

Hot Misal Pav in Virar – A True Maharashtrian Comfort Plate

Something is so pleasurable about a full plate of hot misal pav and this one really fulfils on the flavour, spice and comfort. The tarri (that spicy, reddish gravy) is the hero of the story. It is bold, aromatic and spiced with just the right amount of spice without being too fiery, just like a Maharashtrian one would have.

The pav is tender and slightly toasted which is ideal in absorbing the gravy. Take any piece and put it in the misal, take some farsan and put it in your mouth, a bit of onion and lemon and you have that sweet-spicy-tangy-crunchy bite that makes misal pav so addictive.

Final Rating: 10/10 – Spicy, soulful, and completely satisfying.

Homemade Prawn Fry - Simple, Spicy & Soul-Satisfying

Browned to perfection and smoldering on the edges, topped with an intense and plentiful spice blend, these prawns are appetizing even before the first morsel. The texture is spot on. The prawns are fat and juicy on the inside and the outside has a slight caramel crunch that gives one bite after another satisfaction.

The masala sticks like glue, and provides a medium heat that progresses but not too intensely to overwhelm the natural sweetness of the prawns. They are served with plain steamed rice, a plate of spicy curry and a light sabzi, making the meal look hearty and comforting. It is the type of the plate that makes you remember why homemade food can always be hitting differently.

Final Score: 10/10 - Crispy, spicy, and heartwarming.

Crispy Dosa After Darshan = Pure Bliss

There are meals that hit differently and this dosa is one of them. It is long, golden, and crisp to the last drop and enters the table in the form of work of art. The outer layer is roasted to that perfect tone of brown which is thin and crunchy but strong enough to maintain its Figure.

When you crack it the first time it is great content. This stiffness of exterior yields to the suety of somewhat fluffy interior, forming the ideal unity of feeling. The masala stuffing is friendly and not too spicy, with a warm, homey quality, on the inside, not too weighty, but tasty enough to leave every bite good.

Sides are the only aspects that really make the experience a notch higher. The coconut chutney provides a cool creamy contrast to the dish and the sambar offers warmth and depth of taste. Combined with good company and that after-darshan feeling, this dosa is more than breakfast—it is a comfort on a plate.

Overall Rating: 10/10 - Gold, crispy perfection that cannot be disappointed.

RATING: 9/10

Drizzle, Bite, Repeat - The Dream of a Chocolate Lover!

It just takes one glance at this waffle to know that it would be a gourmet affair. Crispy and golden on the outside and fluffy as a puff on the inside, this waffle makes the ultimate platform around what really makes the difference, that glossy layer of melted chocolate on top, which is a half an inch thick.

The chocolate drizzle does not only serve a decorative purpose but it is rich, smooth and decadently satisfying. Each bite offers a juxtaposition of textures, the slight crunch of the waffle grid, the light and airy interior, and the pieces of fudge chocolate almost brownie-like, generously arranged on the top. It is not so much like a snack but a whole dessert experience.

Even better still is the fact that it is well balanced. There is a substantial chocolate topping though the dessert does not seem to be over sweetened. The waffle itself is light, buttery in flavour and it goes along well with the rich cocoa flavour. The chocolate melts a little in the pockets when it is hot to form little bursts of sticky sweetness in each square.

Final Score: 9/10 - Crispy, chocolate filled and full filling.

RATING: 9/10

Slurp, Smile, Repeat - A Plate That Really Delivers!

The hot plate of freshly tossed noodles is incredibly comforting and this one will not disappoint the hype. Even the initial sight suggests that the glossy covering of the sauce adorning every strand is going to be big flavoured - and it pays off. The noodles are well cooked, tender, but not too soft, and have a bite that is just right, and makes each forkful wholesome.

The balance of flavours is what is really impressive. The soy based savoury sauce is well refined with a light touch of garlic and spice hence that addictive Indo-Chinese street food flavor that everyone desires. It is not too spicy, yet it is spicy enough to stimulate your taste Buds. The vegetables provide texture and contrast to the silky noodles and make it fresh and crunchy. Each bite has layers to it, saucy, with a touch of smoke, and richness.

The wok-tossed smell is added to the whole experience. The slight burnt taste provides a sense of authenticity to it and it feels like it was produced at your local stall probably on the side of the road. Combine this dish with some chilled beverage and a bunch of friends and it is at once not only food, but also a vibe.

Final Rating: 9/10 - Due to its crisp patty, soft bread, and balanced tastes, it is an excellent option for fast-food lovers in the area.

RATING: 8/10

Fried Nation — Mira Road

The Crispy Chicken Burger experience.

We have recently been to the outlet and We gave this trendy item a shot and it came out to be a fairly pleasing experience. The burger was presented in a well-packed pack and appealing immediately. The bun was tender and slightly burnt, which made it fresh and fluffy.

The outstanding feature of this burger is evidently the chicken patty. It was crispy on the outside and tender inside. There was appropriate seasoning, neither too spicy, nor too bland. The chicken was complemented well with the sauce that was creamy and tangy. Fresh lettuce and other fillings also created light crunch to the sandwich. The size of the portion is good and serves as a meal in itself, particularly when accompanied by fries or a drink. It is reasonably priced, making it a good choice for a fast meal.

Final Verdict: 8/10 - Comfort, taste and entertainment in a single plate.
